# NASA SEES Internship -- Sustainable World, Sustainable Future

**Program:** STEM Enhancement in Earth Science (SEES)  
**Institution:** NASA / University of Texas at Austin  
**Mentor:** Debbie Reynolds  
**Date:** Summer 2020

Competitively selected NASA internship focused on Earth and Space Science data analysis, conducted remotely during the COVID-19 pandemic with a globally distributed team of student researchers.

---

## Overview

The NASA SEES program places high school and undergraduate students in NASA-affiliated research experiences centered on Earth science and remote sensing. This internship was conducted during the summer of 2020, at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, which shaped the focus of the project toward sustainability and global resilience.

Working alongside peers from across the United States and internationally, I contributed to the Sustainable World / Sustainable Future project -- an initiative designed to explore how communities and systems could adapt to and recover from the disruptions caused by COVID-19 while maintaining environmental sustainability.

---

## Project Focus

The central question driving our work: how can the world build toward a sustainable future in the context of a global pandemic?

Our team analyzed NASA Earth and Space Science mission datasets to understand environmental changes occurring during the pandemic -- including shifts in air quality, emissions, and land use -- and developed proposals for sustainable recovery pathways.

---

## Key Activities

**Earth Science Data Analysis**  
Used NASA mission datasets to investigate environmental indicators. The pandemic provided a unique natural experiment, with measurable changes in pollution and human activity visible through satellite data.

**Sustainability Project Development**  
Developed a project addressing the intersection of COVID-19 and environmental sustainability, focusing on how disruptions to human systems revealed opportunities for more sustainable practices going forward.

**48-Hour Global Innovation Challenge**  
Participated in a time-constrained international innovation challenge, collaborating across time zones to design a solution aimed at mitigating COVID-19 spread while advancing sustainable practices.

**Python Coursework**  
Completed a foundational Python programming course offered through the program, building skills in data analysis and scientific computing.

**NASA Earth Science Online Course**  
Completed required NASA Earth Science coursework covering remote sensing, satellite data interpretation, and Earth system science fundamentals.

## Program Context

NASA SEES is administered through the University of Texas at Austin and places students with NASA scientists and educators. The program is highly competitive and designed to give students hands-on experience with real NASA datasets and research questions. Participants work in teams, complete structured coursework, and produce original research or design projects.
